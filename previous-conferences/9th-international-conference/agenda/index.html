2020 ICRAT Conference Agenda

The 9th International Conference on Research in Air Transportation (ICRAT) was be held in a virtual format, in a combination of synchronous and asynchronous modes.

The synchronous session was held on Tuesday, September 15, 2020, from 9:00 am to 1:00 pm EDT.

The agenda for the synchronous session was as follows:

Time    
0900 - 0910 Welcome Marc Bourgois, EUROCONTROL
0910 - 0915 Virtual ICRAT Dr. Yu Zhang, University of South Florida
0915 - 0920 Opening Remarks Dr. Robert H. Bishop, Dean of Engineering at the University of South Florida
0920 - 1000 Keynote Speaker Ken Hylander, Executive Vice President and Chief Safety Officer, AMTRAK
1000 – 1005 Doctoral Papers Dr. Seth Young, The Ohio State University
1005 – 1015 Paper Selection Dr. David Lovell, University of Maryland
Dr. Hartmut Fricke, Technische Universität Dresden
